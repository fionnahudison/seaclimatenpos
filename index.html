What is the Southeast Asian Climate NPOs Repository?

This repository aims to document systematically the progress and implementation of Non-Profit Organisations in Southeast Asian countries.

This website contains the information of the various NPO in each country in this region. Click on the "Database" tab and feel free to search for a country or a specific organisation.

If your organisation is not listed or if you have any feedback, do feel free to reach us via the "Contact Us" tab.

Why Non-Profit Organisations?

Despite the great climate potential of NPOs in the fight against climate change, there is significantly less information and data pertaining to this sector. This is especially so in the SEA region, thus creating research and knowledge gaps which this study by the Institute of Environment and Sustainability (IES) aims to fill.
