Save the Earth Cambodia (STEC)

Save the Earth Cambodia (STEC) is a non-government, non-profit, neutral, non-racial, non-religious, and non-political organization. STEC does not provide equipment and financial means and human resources to support any political party or its candidates.Save the Earth promotes environmental & energy facilities, builds local capacity at community and institutional levels and creates awareness in the community and in the local authorities in regard to climate change and gender issues.

Purpose

    We empower institutions and gender responsive community people including marginalized, vulnerable, indigenous etc. on agriculture, health, NRM (water, land, forest, natural resources etc.), sustainable eco-friendly livelihoods to reduce the impacts of climate change and disasters by creating awareness, advocacy for capacity strengthening, gender empowerment; support them to build healthy and environmentally friendly communities and public institutions.

Programmes

  • Building Climate and Disaster Resilient School Communities in Cambodia (School Preparedness programme)
  • Building Climate and Disaster Resilient Communities through Micro Insurance

    Wildlife Conservation Society Cambodia

    The Wildlife Conservation Society, founded in 1895, has the clear mission to save wildlife and wild places across the globe.

    Purpose

    The Wildlife Conservation Society saves wildlife and wild places worldwide. We do so through science, global conservation, education and the management of the world's largest system of urban wildlife parks, led by the flagship Bronx Zoo. Together these activities change attitudes towards nature and help people imagine wildlife and humans living in harmony. WCS is committed to this mission because it is essential to the integrity of life on Earth.

    Programmes

    • Aquaculture project contributes to reduction of illegal fishing
    • Supporting civil society in preah vihear and mondulkiri
    • Resin-tapping in the northern plains
    • REDD activities
    • Keo Seima wildlife sanctuary

    Culture and Environment Preservation Association (CEPA)

    The Culture and Environment Preservation Association (CEPA) is a Cambodian non-government organization (NGO) get official registration with the Ministry of Interior on 10th April 1996. Strategy programmer of CEPA will strongly focused on economic empowerment, rights based approach, advocacy and sustainable livelihoods. So that SUSTAINABLE LIVELIHOOD is the cornerstone of its program strategy.

    Purpose

      Our mission is to improve good governance, sustainable livelihoods, enrich natural environment and equip appropriate skills and technologies for sustainable development in Cambodia.
